ABOUT US

THE BEHEMOTH is a pioneer in independent game development and publishing across a variety of consoles and platforms. We specialize in making memorable experiences that are both fun to play and beautiful to look at! Making stuff with our hands and meeting the people who play our games helps make us into who we are and what we create.
